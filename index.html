The Model

Simple. Sustainable.
Built for Nigerian Schools.

No capital outlay. No hidden fees. Just a genuine partnership that benefits your school, your students, and your community.

01
🏫

We Find Your School

We identify private primary or secondary schools without a functional ICT lab. You apply — we review your school profile and schedule a free visit.

02
🤝

We Sign a BOT Agreement

You provide one room and mains power. We bring everything else — computers, UPS backup, networking, projector, and software. The lab is up in 30 days.

03
💻

Your School Uses the Lab Freely

The lab belongs to EduLab — but your school can use the systems for any school activity during non-training hours. It becomes your school's ICT facility.

04
📚

We Train Your Students

Our certified instructors deliver 2 hours of structured tech training per class weekly — covering digital skills, AI, and intensive CBT exam practice for JAMB, WAEC, and NECO.

05
💰

Students Pay a Small Term Fee

Students pay ₦10,000 per term — a fraction of any textbook bundle. That fee is our revenue. You pay nothing. Your school gets a fully-equipped lab and trained students.

06
🚀

Your Students Gain a Real Edge

By SS2, EduLab students have built real AI/ML projects, practised hundreds of CBT exam questions, and earned a verified digital skills certificate. That is a career head-start.

Who Qualifies

Is Your School
a Good Fit?

We are currently accepting applications from schools in Ondo and Osun States. Here's what we look for.

🏫

Private School

Any registered private primary or secondary school — or a combined primary/secondary school — is eligible to apply.

👥

Minimum 100 Students

Your school should have at least 100 enrolled students to make the programme financially viable for both parties.

🚫

No Functional ICT Lab

Schools without a working computer lab are our priority. If you have one, we can still discuss a curriculum-only partnership.

🔌

A Spare Room + Power

You need one dedicated room of at least 40sqm and a stable mains power connection. We handle everything else.

📍

Ondo or Osun State

We are currently deploying in Ondo and Osun States. Schools in other Southwest states can join the waitlist.

✅

Committed Proprietor

A 3-year BOT partnership requires buy-in from the school proprietor or principal. It is a long-term commitment — and the benefits compound over time.
