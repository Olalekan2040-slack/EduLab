The Model

Simple. Sustainable.
Built to Change Lives.

No capital outlay. No hidden costs. A genuine partnership where schools win, students gain world-class tech skills, and EduLab earns its keep.

1
Selection

We Find Your School

We identify private schools in Ondo and Osun States without a functional ICT lab. You apply — we visit, assess your space, and confirm the partnership within days.

2
Agreement

We Sign a 3-Year BOT Deal

You give us one room and mains power. We install 20 to 50 computers (scaled to your student population), UPS backup, networking, projector, and all software — within 30 days, at no cost to you.

3
Free Use

Your School Uses the Lab Freely

The equipment belongs to EduLab — but your school uses the computers freely during non-training hours for lessons, school exams, admin, or any purpose. It becomes your school's ICT facility in every practical sense.

4
Training

We Train Every Student

Certified EduLab instructors deliver 2 hours of structured tech training per class per week — Python, AI, and machine learning — building real, employable skills alongside intensive CBT practice for JAMB, WAEC, and NECO.

5
Revenue

Students Pay a Small Term Fee

Students pay ₦10,000 per term — less than one textbook bundle. The school pays nothing. That fee is EduLab's revenue. The school gains a world-class lab and trained students at zero cost.

6
Outcome

Students Gain a Real, Lasting Edge

By SS2, EduLab graduates have built real AI and machine learning projects, sat hundreds of CBT mock exams, and hold a verified digital skills certificate — positioning them for tech jobs, freelance income, and global remote opportunities that no other school in their city offers.

Who Qualifies

Is Your School
a Good Fit?

Accepting applications from schools in Ondo and Osun States. Here is what we look for.

1

Private School

Any registered private primary, secondary, or combined school is eligible — regardless of size.

2

100+ Students

At least 100 enrolled students ensures the programme works financially for both parties.

3

No Functional ICT Lab

Schools without a working lab are our first priority. Outdated labs may also qualify.

4

One Room + Power

A dedicated room (at least 40sqm) and mains power. We bring UPS backup — NEPA is not an excuse.

5

Ondo or Osun State

Deploying now in both states. Other Southwest schools can join the expansion waitlist.

6

Committed Proprietor

A 3-year BOT partnership requires genuine leadership buy-in. Schools that are serious rise to the top of our list.
